How to Enter Setup Code for Your New Vizio TV?

Setting up your new Vizio TV is a breeze! Follow these simple steps to enter the setup code and get started:


  • Make sure your TV is powered on and connected to your Wi-Fi network.
  • Find the setup or activation option on your TV's home screen.
  • You'll see a prompt to enter a setup code. This code can be found on your TV screen or in the user manual.
  • Open a web browser on your computer or mobile device and go to Vizio.com/setup.
  • Input the code displayed on your TV into the designated field on the Vizio website.
  • Complete the setup process by following the prompts on your TV and the Vizio website.

To access these features on your Smart TV, Firestick, or any other supported device, you must complete the My5 activation using your activation code at my5.tv/activate.


Register your VIZIO product - VIZIO com Support

To register your VIZIO product, follow these simple steps:

  • Go to the VIZIO Support page.
  • If you already have a VIZIO account, sign in using your credentials. If you don't have an account, you'll need to create one by clicking on the "Create Account" button and following the prompts.
  • Once signed in, navigate to the product registration section. Enter the necessary information about your VIZIO product, including the model number and serial number. This information can usually be found on the back of your device or in the user manual.
  • After filling in all the required details, submit the registration form. You should receive a confirmation email once your product is successfully registered.
